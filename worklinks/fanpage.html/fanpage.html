About Carti

Jordan Terrel Carter, better known by his alias, Playboi Cart is an American rapper and song writer. He gained traction with his first studio mixtape, "Playboi Carti" and broke into the mainstream with his first Debut album, "Die Lit". However, his highest charting album goes to his second studio album, "Whole Lotta Red" which charted at number one on US Billboard Charts. For more oh Jordan and his background, Click here.
